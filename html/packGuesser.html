    HOW TO PLAY?

    Daily guessing games from the game "IdleOn". Games changes at midnight in your timezone.

    The card unblurs slightly for each guess. When you guess correctly, the card will be fully revealed.

    How fast can you discover what card it is?

    Good luck and have fun!

    This Privacy Policy explains how this website collects, uses, and protects personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable EU data protection laws.

    Data Collection

    This website does not directly collect, store, or process personal data of visitors. No user accounts, contact forms, or databases are maintained.

    We do set a small, local cookie to support game UX: idleondle_last_win and per-game cookies named idleondle_win_<game>. These cookies store a JSON payload containing the game identifier, an ISO timestamp of when you completed the daily puzzle, and the number of guesses you used. The cookies are used only locally by the site to show that you've already completed today's puzzle, automatically reveal the answer, and display the guess count in the post-win modal.

    These cookies are non-personal, do not contain any identifiers that would reasonably identify you, are not shared with third parties by this site, and are retained locally for 365 days to allow users to revisit prior wins on the same device. For clarity, this is separate from advertising cookies used by Google AdSense or Umami (see below).
