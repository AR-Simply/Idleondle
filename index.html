    HOW TO PLAY?

    Daily guessing games from the game "IdleOn". Games changes at midnight in your timezone.

    Type in the name of an item and it will reveal its properties. The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the daily item.

    • Green indicates the property is an exact match.
    • Red indicates the property is wrong.
    • ⬇️ ⬆️ indicates if the answer is above or below your guess.

    With each guess you learn more details about the daily item. If you need help, you can unlock a clue about the category of the item after a certain number of guesses.

    INCLUDED ITEMS:

    • Weapons: Fisticuffs, bows, spears, wands
    • Armor: Helmets, pants, pendants, rings, shoes
    • Tools: Pickaxes, hatchets, fishing rods, nets, traps, worship skulls

    PROPERTIES:

    • Class: Which class can equip the item.
    • Level Req: What level is required to equip the item.
    • Power: Any kind of power on the item, such as attack power, mining power, catching power and so on.
    • Stats: The amount of a stat on the item.
    • Sell price: How much the item sells for.
    • Source: Where the item comes from.

    Good luck and have fun!
